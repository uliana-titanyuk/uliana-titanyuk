THERE ARE WILD AND DOMESTIC ANIMALS. WILD ANIMALS LIVE IN THE FOREST OR IN THE ZOO. SOME ANIMALS ARE DANGEROUS. A FOX, A WOLF, A BEAR, A HARE, A LAMA,  A TIGER, AN ELEPHANT ARE WILD ANIMALS. DOMESTIC ANIMALS LIVE AT HOME OR AT THE FARM. THEY ARE DOGS, CATS, RABBITS, COWS, HORSES, PARROTS. ANIMALS THAT WE HAVE AT HOME ARE CALLED PETS.
